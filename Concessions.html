Food and Drinks 

At Sam's Theatre of Flix, we understand that a movie experience is not complete without some delicious snacks and refreshing drinks. That is why we offer a wide variety of food and beverage options for our guests to enjoy. From classic popcorn and soda to gourmet sandwiches and craft beer, we have something for everyone. Our concession stand is conveniently located in the lobby area and is open before and during the movie. So, sit back, relax, and indulge in your favorite treats while enjoying the show.
